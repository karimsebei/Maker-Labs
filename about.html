WHAT MAKES you WANT TO JOIN US ?

  • Founded on Oct 03, 2014
  • In ISTIC

MAKER LABS is much more than a club. It is a thriving community of passionate students from the Higher Institute of Information and Communication Technologies (ISTIC) founded in 2014, all united by their love for new technologies, especially in the field of robotics. We have chosen the theme of the Vikings for the first edition of our event ISTIC ROBOTS, with the aim of infusing creative and innovative potential into our participants. We look forward to a day filled with innovation, competition, and fun. Good luck to all participants!
